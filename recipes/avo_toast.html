Avocado Toast

Description

Avocado toast is an Australian classic, seen on brunch menus everywhere

It's a simple, quick recipe perfect for breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 1 Avocado
  • Sourdough bread
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Optional: Cherry tomatoes
  • Optional: Feta cheese

Steps

  1. Slice and toast the sourdough.
  2. Smash the avocado by crushing with a fork.
  3. Spread the avocado on the toasted bread, adding the salt, pepper, and other ingredients to taste.
